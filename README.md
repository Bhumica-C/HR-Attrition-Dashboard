# HR Attrition Interactive Dashboard 
— SkillCraft Technology Internship

## Project Overview
This project involves building an interactive 
HR Attrition dashboard using Power BI on the 
IBM HR Employee Attrition dataset to understand 
why employees are leaving the organization.

## Business Problem
A company's HR team needs to understand:
- Which departments have highest attrition?
- Does age or salary affect attrition?
- Which job roles are most at risk?
- How can we retain valuable employees?

## Dataset
- Source: IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition
- Rows: 1,470 employees
- Columns: 35 features including Age, Department,
  JobRole, MonthlyIncome, Attrition

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX formulas
- Power Query Editor

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards (Total Employees, Income, Attrition)
- Attrition by Department (Bar Chart)
- Attrition by Age Group (Column Chart)
- Donut Chart (Attrition by Gender)
- Treemap (Attrition by Job Role)
- Scatter Plot (Age vs Monthly Income)
- Gauge Chart (Average Monthly Income)
- Interactive Slicers (Department, Gender, Age)

## Key Insights
1. Sales department has highest attrition rate
2. Younger employees (25-35) leave more often
3. Low monthly income correlates with attrition
4. Lab Technicians and Sales Executives 
   are most at risk
5. Male employees show slightly higher attrition
